OUR STORY

Redefining the industry’s approach to specialty chemicals distribution.



Right from the start, our goal is clear. Drive the industry forward by being your reliable partner in gaining access to specialty chemicals that will improve current technologies.

Our innovative roots arise from our partner company, ChemPrise. And we are moving forward with the values, expertise, and extensive knowledge we’ve accumulated by working with them for years. As an intelligent specialty chemicals company, we are firm believers that improving the quality of end-users’ products will be our way towards keeping costs at a minimum and value at the maximum.

We work as an established team of innovators, and we have built new and maintained existing relationships with different global companies that have extensive background knowledge in chemistry. Combining this with our in-depth knowledge of the industry, we are creating a guarantee for you to not only find quality specialty materials at reasonable prices, but also offer end-to-end solutions.

As a material agnostic team of experts, we aim to implement our materials into your current technologies so you can become a vital part in moving science forward. We collaborate with our clientele to provide solutions to their specific problems. By looking at the bigger picture, we act on offering help with the entire bills of materials, provide multiple levels of bill for materials, and ultimately shorten the development timeframe.

These are just a few of the many actions we plan on taking to bridge the gap between markets and move forward with our clients with unstoppable force.
